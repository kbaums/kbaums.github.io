Film Production

FMP 300 - Film Production: Film & Media

This was probably my most stressful project to date. I worked on my short film for four months. I wrote the script, gathered talent and crew, organized shoot days, directed and edited the entire film.


Looking back, there are definitely things I want to change but I suppose I would think that no matter what because this was my first go at film production. I look forward to working on my next short film in any role in production.
