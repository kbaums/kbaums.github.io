3D Sludge Monster

CPI 421 - 3D Modeling and Texturing

I designed, modeled, and animated a sludge monster using 3Ds Max, Mudbox, and MotionBuilder. I used motion capture data from Carnegie Mellon University to animate the monster. This was the first time I animated a 3D character with motion capture data.

Game Concept Art

CPI 321 - Fundamentals of Game Art

This course taught the basics of creating concept art for games. It focused heavily on using Photoshop to paint images. I had never used Photoshop in this capacity before, nor had I relied so heavily on a pen and tablet to draw.


The images in this gallery show my work in learning to paint in Photoshop. I hope to continue building my skills here. Hopefully, I will find time to do so!
